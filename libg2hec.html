G2HEC: A Genus 2 Crypto C++ Library

The G2HEC (Genus 2 HyperElliptic Curve) is a free portable C++ library providing divisor group operations in the Jacobian of a genus 2 (imaginary) hyperelliptic curve over finite fields. It is useful for programmers and researchers in mathematics and cryptography.
